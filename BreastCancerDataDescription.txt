BreastCancerData description
##############################

Simulated Data:  Oncotype DX marker in the breast cancer trial. 
  358 observations with the following 30 variables. 

trt: treatment assignment (trt = 1  chemo + tamoxifen/CAF-T; trt = 0 tamoxifen alone )

D: five-year cancer recurrence or death (D = 1 is a bad outcome)

RS: Recurrence Score

blackrace: indicator for African American racial status 

nodes4: indicator for number of positive nodes (1-3 vs 4) 

pgr: ER/PgR status ([PR-neg and ER-pos]  versus [PR-pos and (ER-pos or ER-neg)]

surgtime: Time of surgery (<= 6 weeks versus > 6 weeks)

tumsiz: Tumor size (< 2cm; 2-5 cm; >=5cm), coded 1,2,3 resp. 

age: age at randomization (years)

grade: tumor grade 

eraxis, her2axis, invasionaxis, prolaxis: axis values 

BAG1_2 BCL2_2 CCNB1_2 CD68_2 CEGP1_2 CTSL2_1 ESTR1_1 GRB7_2 GSTM1_1 HER2_3 KI_67_2 MYBL2_1 PR_6 STK15_2 STMY3_3 SURV_2: gene expression values
